What is Farm Shack?

Here at Farm Shack we ensure to provide the authenticity of a farm house resturant/resturant. Our Shop is located in the heart of the University of West england with sources of farm land in the local area. We are a inclusive shop and welcome everyone in through our doors! Our shop is the first place youll enter which homes all fresh fruit and veg locally sourced at a fair price. After making you way through the shop, you'll be faced with the award winning resturant. Our sit in resturant has a variety of meals fit for anyone above the age of 5! This includes burgers, pizzas, vegetarian dishes all with ingredients sourced from our farm!
